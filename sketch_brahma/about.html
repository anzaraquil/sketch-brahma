About Us.

We, the people at Sketch Brahma, love to partner with the growing startups and leading companies of the world to deliver the highest value of satisfaction on branding, UI/UX design, mobile and web development. We come from different backgrounds, different lifestyles but with a common purpose of building a strong design framework and delivering a consistent digital product experience for our clients.

We came into existence in 2017 and are based out of Bengaluru, India. We started with Ather Energy as one of our early clients and have since collaborated with a few growing startups like ZestMoney, Licious, Mfine, Perfios, Shipway and established brands like Airtel and Mahindra Comviva. We strive to deliver maximum value by incorporating best operational efficiency from the super energetic team with strong niche skills.
